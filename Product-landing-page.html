FLUTE GUIDE FOR BEGINNERS

How to play the flute correctly

The posture used to play the flute isn't complicated at all.

Remember that you can correct your posture over time.

Repertoires for flute

There are many pieces for flute, most of them are beautiful!

You can start playing "twinkle twinkle little star" or something like that to begin with.

Studies and scales

You need to study hard to master the art of playing the flute, but don't worry!

You must practise scales and exercises, most of them are boring, but really useful.
